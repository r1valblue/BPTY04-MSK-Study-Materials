🔗 Arthrology – Elbow Joint Complex
3 joints enclosed in one capsule:
  • Humeroradial joint – Spheroidal (ball and socket)
  • Humeroulnar joint – Hinge (uniaxial)
  • Proximal radioulnar joint – Pivot
🧠 CRAzy TULips: Capitulum = RAdius · Trochlea = ULna

🔗 Annular Ligament & Bursae
Annular ligament: Surrounds the radial head, inserting at both ends into the radial notch of the ulna. Provides stability to the proximal radioulnar joint.

Bursae

📌 Common Tendon Groups
Common Flexor Tendon (CFT) – medial epicondyle:
Pronator Teres · FCR · Palmaris Longus · FCU · FDS
All except Pronator Teres are wrist flexors
Common Extensor Tendon (CET) – lateral epicondyle:
ECRB · Extensor Digitorum · EDM · ECU
All are wrist extensors
⚠️ Lateral epicondyle tenderness = "Tennis Elbow" (common extensor tendon overload). Medial = "Golfer's Elbow".
